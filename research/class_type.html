Research Statement

In the past years, I was fortunate enough to be in contact with many brilliant researchers who introduced me to a variety of research topics and influenced (and still have an impact) my research. I currently have different, but related research interests:

  • Proof Theory and Logical Frameworks
  • Foundations of Computer Security
  • Denotational Semantics of Programming Languages
  • Formal Methods applied to Networking

More details can be found here.
